# MedAssist Web Support for Elderly Patients Visiting Hospitals Alone

## Project Overview

MedAssist is a web-based support tool designed for elderly patients who need to navigate hospital visits alone. The goal is to make complex hospital environments and multi-step medical processes easier to understand through simple, elderly-friendly web guidance.

This project focuses on helping users move through key hospital tasks such as registration, finding departments, waiting, testing, payment, follow-up consultation, and medication pickup. The website is intended to reduce confusion, missed steps, and navigation errors during solo hospital visits.

## Problem Summary

Older adults who need to visit hospitals alone often struggle with complex spaces, unclear signage, fragmented procedures, and hard-to-understand medical information. This makes tasks like registration, finding departments, payment, testing, and medication pickup confusing, slow, and physically exhausting, especially for users with reduced vision, hearing, memory, or mobility.

This is a real problem based on prior field research, including observation of elderly patients in hospital settings and interviews with older adults who visited hospitals alone.

## Desired Outcome

Users can use a simple, elderly-friendly website to understand where they are in the hospital process, what they need to do next, and where they need to go. The system should reduce confusion, missed steps, and navigation errors while helping older adults complete solo hospital visits with more confidence and independence.

## Solution Overview

This project proposes a web-based support system for elderly patients navigating hospital visits alone. The website should provide:

- step-by-step visit guidance
- simple explanations of hospital tasks
- clear indication of the current step and next step
- department and destination finding support
- elderly-friendly interface design with low cognitive load

Rather than replacing hospital systems, this website adds a clearer and more accessible support layer on top of existing hospital processes.

## AI Feature

The website may include an AI-assisted photo understanding feature. Users can take or upload photos of hospital signs, service windows, locations, or medical documents, and the system will interpret them in context and translate them into simple, elderly-friendly next-step guidance.

Examples:
- “You are now at the payment area. Next, go to the laboratory on the second floor.”
- “This is a test order. You need to pay before joining the queue.”
- “This sign shows the cardiology department is to the left.”
